# CTF Preparation Series


## 👋 Welcome!

Capture The Flags (CTFs) are gamified competitive cybersecurity events that are based on different challenges or aspects of information security. They are excellent for both beginners and experienced hackers looking to develop, test, and prove their skills because they gamify hacking concepts. [¹](https://www.hackthebox.com/blog/what-is-ctf)

The goal of this preparation series is to introduce basic concepts and tools of information security in order to capacitate the participants in resolving beginner-level CTF challenges.

The content of this preparation series is based on Mastercard Capture the Flag Series [²](https://ncc-cnc.ca/ctfseries/) event recommended learning. The goal is to **introduce beginners** to CTF challenges.
